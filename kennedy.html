John F. Kennedy

John F. Kennedy was the 35th president of the United States of America, he served from 1961 to the date of his assination. This was during the time of the height of the cold war. John F. Kennedy was born on may 29, 1917 in Brookline Massachusetts, he did his early education in schools near the boston area. For college he attended Harvard where he earned a Bacholors of art in government with a focus on international affairs. During this time he wrote Why England Slept where he talked about England's unwillingness to strengthen its military before world war 2, he also called for an anglo-american alliance to stop the spread of totalitarian powers. Though Kennedy planned on going to Yale's law program after stanford he wasn't able to as world war two rolled around and then he just joined the navy instead. He became an officer and commanded 5 PT(patrol torpedo) boats. During his time with the military he racked up 6 awards among them a purple heart. Kennedy first joined politics by becoming a member of the house of representatives in 1947, some of policies were better housing for veterans, health care for all, health care at work, and opposition to the soviet union. He spent six years as a member of the house of representatives and in this time he was notably anti communist as he supported the Truman doctrine(an American foreign policy that originated with the primary goal of containing Soviet geopolitical expansion during the Cold War.) and wanted all communists to register with the government. Kennedy then sought to run for the senate position for Massachusetts in the 1952 election, with the support of his family he was able to win despite there being a heavy majority of opposition voters in terms for the presidential vote. He was reelected as a senator once and then he ran for the presidential position in the 1960 elections. Though he was faced with some hard opposition Kennedy was able to become the democratic party's nominee for president. He chose Lyndon B. Johnson as his vice-president. He won one of the closest elections in US history against Richard Nixon. With his win he became the youngest person to ever be elected president at 43 years of age. Most of Kennedy’s work in foreign affairs quite obviously had to do with the soviet union and the cold war. One major thing that happened was his summit with the leader of the soviet union Nikita Khrushev, in this summit he carved out the deal for the treaty of west and east berlin. Another thing that happened was the Bay of Pigs invasion which was a massive failed attempt to overthrow Castro in Cuba, Kennedy had not provided any support to the invading forces and it made him look bad. He also had to deal with the Cuban missile crisis where Russian nukes were in Cuba among other things. JFK was assassinated on november 22 1963 while taking a political trip though Dallas, Texas.
